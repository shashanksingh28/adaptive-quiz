Statements - The continue and break statements
At times, you would like to re-iterate a loop without executing the remaining statement within the loop. The continue statement causes the loop to re-iterate and start over from the top most statement inside the loop.Where there is an ability to re-iterate the loop, there is an ability to exit the loop when required.
